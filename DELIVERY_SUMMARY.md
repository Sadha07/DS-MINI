## 📊 Key Findings

### Main Discoveries

**What matters most for winning:**
1. **Defensive efficiency** - Keeping opponents' score low
2. **Offensive efficiency** - Scoring points efficiently
3. **Ball handling** - Minimizing turnovers

**What surprisingly doesn't matter much:**
- 3-point shooting percentage (contradicts modern basketball intuition)

### Model Insights
- Team performance is multifactorial (no single stat determines wins)
- Statistics can predict ~15.5% average error in win percentage
- Offensive and defensive ratings most important
- Regression coefficients show relative importance of each factor
